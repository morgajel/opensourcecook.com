**Ingredients:**  
6 chopped apples  
6 chopped snickers candy bars  
1/2 cup chopped pecans  
1 (12oz) pkg of whipped cream

**Directions:**  
Combine apples, snickers and pecans; mix well.  Fold in whipped topping and refrigerate.  Best served the same day made.
